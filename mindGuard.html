// Why MindGuard exists

Awareness first, stigma last.

Most people wait years before talking to anyone about what they're carrying. MindGuard exists to close that gap a little — with plain-language information, tools you can use in under two minutes, and a clear path to real support.

We don't diagnose, sell anything, or track you. This page works the same with or without an internet connection, on purpose.

MindGuard provides general educational information and is not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always seek the advice of a qualified provider with questions about a mental health condition.
